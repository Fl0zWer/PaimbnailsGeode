# Paimbnails

Developed by <cg>F</c><cg>l</c><cl>o</c><cl>z</c><cl>W</c><cg>e</c><cg>r</c> — [Profile](user:17046382)

Paimbnails is a mod focused on improving the Geometry Dash experience with <cl>thumbnails</c>, <co>in-game capture</c>, and <cp>moderation utilities</c>.

It includes <cg>optimized downloading</c>, <cy>local caching</c>, and <cj>configurable visual effects</c>.

## Main Features

- <cb>**Level thumbnails**</c>: previews in lists and related screens.
- <cb>**Profile thumbnails**</c>: profile visuals powered by the same thumbnail pipeline.
- <co>**In-game capture (Direct/Render)**</c>: capture flow to generate thumbnails from inside the game.
- <cg>**Optimized downloads**</c>: concurrent downloads + local cache to reduce load times and network usage.
- <cp>**Visual effects**</c>: animated gradients, particles, hover effects, and configurable styles.
- <cj>**Level info screen**</c>: background styles (including “pixel/blur/effects”-style modes).
- <cl>**Settings & tools**</c>: UI tweaks, customization options, and performance settings (e.g. Android safe mode).
- <cp>**Dynamic Song**</c>: Play the level's song while browsing (enable in settings).
- <co>**Layer Editor**</c>: Arrange and hide layers when editing thumbnails to get the perfect shot.
